Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

A simple recipe for delicious, vegan chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Earth Balance vegan butter
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/8 cup almond milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/8 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • vegan semi-sweet chocolate morsels to taste

Steps

  1. Combine butter and sugars with an electronic mixer on a low setting just until well mixed.
  2. Add almond milk and vanilla extract, combine again just until well mixed.
  3. Add flour and baking soda, combine again just until well mixed.
  4. Add chocolate chips and combine by hand or with a large spoon until evenly dispersed.
  5. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper.
  6. Roll into balls (or use a cookie dough scoop) slightly over an inch in diameter and place on parchment paper with roughly 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 inch spacing between cookies.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for ~12 minutes or until very lightly brown on the edges.
