Vegan Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

A simple recipe for delicious, vegan oatmeal raisin cookies.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Earth Balance vegan butter
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened apple sauce
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 3/4 cup raisins (or to taste)

Steps:

  1. Combine butter and sugars with an electronic mixer on a low setting just until well mixed.
  2. Add apple sauce and vanilla extract, and combine again just until well mixed.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, and cinnamon, and combine again just until well mixed.
  4. Add oats, and combine again just until well mixed.
  5. Add raisins and combine by hand or with a large spoon until evenly dispersed.
  6. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Roll into balls (or use a cookie dough scoop) slightly over an inch in diameter and place on parchment paper with roughly 1 1/4 to 1 1/2 inch spacing between cookies.
  8. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-12 minutes or until very lightly brown on the edges.
